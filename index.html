Tottenham Hotspur Football Club

About Tottenham Hotspur

Tottenham Hotspur is a professional football club based in North London, England. The team competes in the Premier League, the highest level of football in England. The club was founded in 1882 and is commonly known as Spurs.

Tottenham is known for its long history and strong fan base. The club plays its home matches at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, one of the most modern stadiums in Europe. Spurs have built a reputation for playing exciting and attacking football over the years.

Achievements

  • FA Cup wins
  • League Cup wins
  • European trophies

Most Recent Achievement

The most recent major trophy won by Tottenham was the League Cup in 2008.
