Tottenham Hotspur Football Club Tottenham Hotspur is a professional football club based in North London, England. The team competes in the Premier League, which is the highest level of football in England. The club was founded in 1882 and is commonly known as Spurs. Tottenham is known for its long history and strong fan base. The club plays its home matches at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, one of the most modern stadiums in Europe.
